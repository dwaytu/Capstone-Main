# SENTINEL Acceptable Use Policy

Effective Date: 2026-03-28
Version: 2026-03-28

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to all users of SENTINEL across web, desktop, and mobile runtimes.

## 2. Allowed Use

You may use SENTINEL only for authorized agency operations, including scheduling, incident handling, asset management, mission support, compliance workflows, and approved reporting.

## 3. Prohibited Conduct

You must not:

- Access data or actions outside assigned permissions.
- Share accounts or credentials with any other person.
- Upload malicious code, scripts, or unauthorized tools.
- Attempt to probe, scan, exploit, or disrupt services.
- Tamper with logs, evidence records, or audit trails.
- Use SENTINEL to harass, discriminate against, or harm individuals.
- Use SENTINEL data for personal gain or unauthorized disclosure.

## 4. Data Handling Expectations

Users must handle operational and personal data responsibly.

Sensitive information must be accessed only when required for duty and must not be copied or exported outside approved channels.

## 5. Device and Network Use

Use only trusted devices and approved networks where possible.

Report lost devices, suspicious activity, or unauthorized login attempts immediately.

## 6. Monitoring and Enforcement

SENTINEL activity may be monitored and audited for security, compliance, and operational continuity.

Violations may result in:

- Access restriction or suspension
- Administrative investigation
- Disciplinary action
- Legal action when applicable

## 7. Policy Updates

This policy may be revised as legal, operational, or security requirements evolve.

Continued platform use indicates acceptance of the current version.

## 8. Contact

Questions about acceptable use should be directed to the authorized system administrator.
