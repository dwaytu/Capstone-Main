# SENTINEL Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-03-28
Version: 2026-03-28

## 1. Purpose

This Privacy Policy explains how SENTINEL processes personal and operational data for authorized security operations.

## 2. Data We Process

Depending on role and workflow, SENTINEL may process:

- Account identifiers (name, username, email, role)
- Contact and profile data
- Authentication and session metadata
- Duty schedules, attendance, and assignment records
- Incident, mission, and support-ticket records
- Asset records (firearms, permits, armored cars, maintenance)
- Audit and access logs
- Location and tracking data when location consent is granted

## 3. Why We Process Data

Data processing supports:

- Identity and access control
- Operational planning and execution
- Compliance and auditability
- Incident response and safety monitoring
- Service reliability and platform security

## 4. Legal and Organizational Basis

Processing is performed for legitimate organizational security operations and compliance obligations.

Data access is role-restricted and logged.

## 5. Location Data

Location data is processed only when location consent is enabled by the user and required for operational features.

Users may decline or disable location consent where allowed, but this may limit location-dependent workflows.

## 6. Sharing and Disclosure

SENTINEL does not provide public data-sharing features.

Data may be disclosed only to authorized personnel, administrators, or legal/compliance authorities when required by law or policy.

## 7. Retention

Data is retained according to organizational and legal requirements for security operations, audit, and compliance.

Retention periods may vary by record type.

## 8. Security Controls

SENTINEL applies technical and administrative safeguards, including:

- Authentication and role-based authorization
- Session controls and token rotation
- Request monitoring and audit logging
- Rate limiting and security header protections
- Environment-based deployment controls

No system can guarantee absolute security. Users should follow organizational security policy.

## 9. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

- Providing accurate data where required
- Protecting account credentials
- Reporting suspected unauthorized access promptly

## 10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect legal, operational, or security changes.

Updated versions become effective on publication.

## 11. Contact

For privacy requests or policy questions, contact the authorized system administrator.
